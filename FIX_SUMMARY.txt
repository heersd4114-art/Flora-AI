⚠️ KNOWN LIMITATIONS & NOTES:

1. Gemini Quota Resets Daily
   - Free tier: 20 requests/day
   - Resets at midnight UTC
   - Status: Currently exhausted
   - Workaround: Use local AI (automatic)

2. Local AI Less Accurate
   - ResNet-50: ~75% accuracy (plant category)
   - Gemini: ~95% accuracy (specific disease)
   - Trade-off: Accuracy vs Availability

3. First Scan Takes Longer
   - First time: 30-60 seconds (loads ResNet-50)
   - Subsequent scans: 5-10 seconds (cached)
   - This is normal and only happens once per session

4. WiFi Network Required
   - Both phone & laptop must be on same network
   - Works on: Home WiFi, Office WiFi, Hotspot
   - Does NOT work: Cellular-only, different networks

5. Phone Must Be Unlocked
   - WiFi connection works even in background
   - But app must be in focus for scanning
